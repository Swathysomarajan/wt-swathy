About

Established in 2014, the Department of Computer Applications is committed to providing quality education in the field of computer science. We currently offer a three-year undergraduate Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) course, equipping students with the necessary skills to thrive in the ever-evolving world of technology.

Facilities

Software Lab
The Department of Computer Applications has a software lab with 30 computers for doing lab work/projects for students. All the computers in the lab run on GNU/Linux OS and have enough applications to help students do the lab/project work.
Department Library
Apart from the college library, the department library has books to help the students learn different subjects. Technical and quantitative aptitude training books are also available in the department library.
